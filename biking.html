Biking

Preparation

When going biking being prepared is always a nessesity. Carrying some of these items could exponentially improve your experience while biking. These items include but are not limited to:

  • Contact Information
  • Food and water
  • Technology
  • Repair tools

Contact Inforamtion

Carrying contact info in your backpack or pocket could safe your life if you get into an accident.

  • Your address
  • Name
  • Emergency contact

Food and Water

Bking is a good workout, staying hydrated and nourished will keep you from getting tired.

  • Water
  • Gatorade
  • Granola bars
  • Fruits
  • Trail Mix

Technology

Staying connected not only will find you the best route but also can get you out of sticky situations if you get lost.

  • Phone
  • Gps
  • Smart watch

Repair Tools

Sometimes accidents happen, staying prepared will keep you on the road instead of calling for help

  • Air pump
  • Extra inner tubes
  • Wheel patch kit
  • Tire lever
  • Multi-tool
